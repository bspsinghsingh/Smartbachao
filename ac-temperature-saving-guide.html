🌡️ AC 24°C vs 26°C vs 28°C — How Much Bill Difference?

क्या सच में temperature बढ़ाने से bill कम होता है? BEE officially क्या कहती है? Calculator से देखें — अपनी setting पर exact saving।

Does increasing temperature really reduce the bill? What does BEE officially say? See with calculator — exact saving on your setting.

🗓 Last Updated: May 2026 ✍️ SmartBachao Editorial Team 📋 Source: BEE/PIB India
⚡ Quick Answer — BEE Official Position
24°C = BEE recommended setting — Bureau of Energy Efficiency India officially यही कहती है। हर 1°C temperature बढ़ाने पर approximately 6% electricity बचती है। 18°C से 24°C करने पर approximately 36% बचत। Source: BEE/PIB India
24°C = BEE recommended setting — Bureau of Energy Efficiency India officially states this. Every 1°C increase saves approximately 6% electricity. Going from 18°C to 24°C saves approximately 36%. Source: BEE/PIB India
❌ MYTH #1 — सबसे Common
"16°C पर set करो — कमरा जल्दी ठंडा होगा"
"Set to 16°C — room will cool faster"
✅ FACT — Science
AC एक constant rate पर cool करती है — thermostat setting से cooling speed नहीं बदलती। 16°C और 24°C पर room same speed से ठंडा होता है। फर्क यह है: 16°C target तक पहुँचने में compressor ज्यादा देर चलता है = ज्यादा electricity। 24°C पर target जल्दी मिलता है, compressor rest करता है = less electricity।
An AC cools at a constant rate — thermostat setting doesn't change cooling speed. Room cools at the same speed at 16°C and 24°C. The difference: at 16°C the compressor runs longer to reach the target = more electricity. At 24°C the target is reached faster, compressor rests = less electricity.
❌ MYTH #2
"24°C पर AC चलाओ तो नींद नहीं आएगी — बहुत गर्म है"
"If AC is at 24°C you won't sleep — it's too warm"
✅ FACT
Sleep के लिए ideal temperature 24-26°C है — यह medically proven है। BLDC ceiling fan 24°C को 22°C जैसा feel करवाता है air circulation से। 16-18°C पर सोने से dry throat, body ache, और immune system issues हो सकते हैं।
The ideal sleep temperature is 24-26°C — this is medically proven. A BLDC ceiling fan makes 24°C feel like 22°C through air circulation. Sleeping at 16-18°C can cause dry throat, body aches, and immune system issues.

BEE 24°C क्यों recommend करती है?Why does BEE recommend 24°C?+

BEE के अनुसार 24°C पर AC compressor efficiently cycle करती है — ज्यादा rest करती है और ज्यादा काम नहीं करती। इससे electricity कम खर्च होती है। साथ ही 24°C human body के लिए comfortable temperature range में है। यह recommendation PIB (Press Information Bureau) India ने भी endorse की है।

According to BEE, at 24°C the AC compressor cycles efficiently — rests more and doesn't overwork. This uses less electricity. Also, 24°C is within the comfortable temperature range for the human body. This recommendation has also been endorsed by PIB (Press Information Bureau) India.

क्या 28°C पर AC useful है?Is AC useful at 28°C?+

हाँ — अगर ceiling fan strong है तो 28°C बहुत comfortable हो सकता है। Humidity कम हो तो especially। Monsoon में 28°C + dehumidify mode excellent होता है। Peak summer (45°C+ outside) में 26°C ज्यादा practical है।

Yes — if you have a strong ceiling fan, 28°C can be very comfortable. Especially when humidity is low. During monsoon, 28°C + dehumidify mode is excellent. In peak summer (45°C+ outside), 26°C is more practical.
