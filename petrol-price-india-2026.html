2026 में petrol prices का issue बहुत serious हो गया है। Iran war की वजह से Strait of Hormuz disrupted है — India का 90% oil यहाँ से आता है। Crude oil $107-111/barrel है। Rupee ₹96/dollar पर है। इन सब का मतलब है — आने वाले समय में fuel prices और बढ़ सकती हैं।

In 2026, petrol prices have become a very serious issue. The Iran war has disrupted the Strait of Hormuz — through which 90% of India's oil comes. Crude oil is at $107-111/barrel. The Rupee is at ₹96/dollar. All this means — fuel prices may rise further in the coming period.

Petrol बचाने के 10 Smart तरीके — 2026

10 Smart Ways to Save Petrol — 2026

1
PM Modi की appeal follow करें Follow PM Modi's Appeal Metro, bus, carpool, cycle, WFH — Modi जी ने specifically इनकी बात की। यह देशभक्ति भी है और personal savings भी। Metro, bus, carpool, cycle, WFH — Modi specifically mentioned these. It's both patriotism and personal savings.
2
Tyre pressure weekly check करें Check Tyre Pressure Weekly Under-inflated tyres 5-10% ज्यादा fuel खाते हैं। Petrol pump पर free में check होता है। 2 minutes — बड़ी saving। Under-inflated tyres consume 5-10% more fuel. Checked free at petrol pumps. 2 minutes — big saving.
3
Smooth Driving — Sudden acceleration नहीं Smooth Driving — No Sudden Acceleration Aggressive driving 20-30% ज्यादा fuel खाती है। Smooth और steady speed — same destination, less fuel। Aggressive driving consumes 20-30% more fuel. Smooth and steady speed — same destination, less fuel.
4
AC कम use करें Use AC Less Car AC 5-15% ज्यादा fuel खाती है। Slow city traffic में windows खोलें। Highway पर windows बंद रखें। Car AC consumes 5-15% more fuel. Open windows in slow city traffic. Keep windows closed on highway.
5
EV या CNG switch plan करें Plan EV or CNG Switch Petrol prices बढ़ती रहेंगी — यह long-term trend है। अभी plan करें। हमारा EV vs Petrol guide देखें। Petrol prices will keep rising — this is a long-term trend. Plan now. Check our EV vs Petrol guide.
