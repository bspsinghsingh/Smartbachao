PPF क्या है और क्यों है यह खास?

What is PPF and Why is it Special?

PPF (Public Provident Fund) एक government-backed savings scheme है। यह India के safest investment options में से एक है। इसकी खासियत है EEE status — मतलब invest करने पर tax deduction (80C), interest पर कोई tax नहीं, और maturity पर भी tax नहीं।

PPF (Public Provident Fund) is a government-backed savings scheme. It's one of India's safest investment options. Its special feature is EEE status — meaning tax deduction on investment (80C), no tax on interest, and no tax at maturity either.

PPF का पैसा बीच में निकाल सकते हैं?Can you withdraw PPF money in the middle?+

5 साल बाद partial withdrawal possible है — certain conditions पर। Medical emergency, education के लिए। Loan भी मिल सकता है 3rd से 6th year के बीच। Full withdrawal सिर्फ maturity (15 साल) पर।

Partial withdrawal is possible after 5 years — under certain conditions. For medical emergency, education. Loan is also available between 3rd and 6th year. Full withdrawal only at maturity (15 years).

PPF rate बदलता रहता है?Does the PPF rate keep changing?+

हाँ — government हर quarter PPF rate review करती है। Last several years से 7.1% है। Rate बदलने पर existing balance पर नया rate लागू होता है। लेकिन यह government-backed है — rate कभी भी बहुत कम नहीं होती।

Yes — the government reviews PPF rate every quarter. It has been 7.1% for the last several years. When rate changes, the new rate applies to the existing balance. But since it's government-backed — the rate never becomes too low.
