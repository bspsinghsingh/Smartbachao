Inflation क्या होती है — Simple Explanation

What is Inflation — Simple Explanation

Inflation का मतलब है — पैसों की purchasing power का कम होना। आज ₹100 में जो मिलता था, अगले साल ₹102-103 देने पड़ सकते हैं। यह normal है — लेकिन जब inflation ज्यादा होती है तो आम आदमी की जेब पर असर पड़ता है।

Inflation means the decreasing purchasing power of money. What you could buy for ₹100 today may cost ₹102-103 next year. This is normal — but when inflation is high, it impacts common people's pockets.

The 2.75% inflation of January 2026 could change in the post-Iran war scenario. Crude oil is at $107-111/barrel. Rupee is at ₹96/dollar. Both these factors increase the likelihood of rising fuel and food inflation in coming months.

India में inflation क्यों बढ़ती है?Why does inflation rise in India?+

मुख्य कारण: fuel prices बढ़ना (crude oil import), food prices बढ़ना (monsoon failure, export बंद), rupee कमजोर होना (imported goods महंगे), demand बढ़ना (economy grow करती है तो prices बढ़ती हैं)। Iran war जैसी global events भी directly affect करती हैं।

Main reasons: rising fuel prices (crude oil import), rising food prices (monsoon failure, export ban), weak rupee (imported goods expensive), rising demand (prices rise as economy grows). Global events like the Iran war also directly affect inflation.

RBI inflation कैसे control करती है?How does RBI control inflation?+

RBI का main tool है repo rate। Inflation बढ़ने पर RBI repo rate बढ़ाती है → banks को borrowing महंगी होती है → loans महंगे होते हैं → लोग कम borrowing और spending करते हैं → demand कम → prices stabilize। यह process 6-12 months लेती है।

RBI's main tool is the repo rate. When inflation rises, RBI raises repo rate → borrowing becomes expensive for banks → loans get expensive → people borrow and spend less → lower demand → prices stabilize. This process takes 6-12 months.
