FD क्या है और कहाँ करें?

What is FD and Where to Do It?

Fixed Deposit (FD) सबसे popular और safe investment है India में। Bank में पैसे deposit करो — fixed tenure के लिए fixed return। Guaranteed return, no market risk। Emergency fund और short-term savings के लिए best।

Fixed Deposit (FD) is India's most popular and safe investment. Deposit money in a bank — fixed return for a fixed tenure. Guaranteed return, no market risk. Best for emergency fund and short-term savings.

FD के interest पर कितना tax लगता है?How much tax on FD interest?+

FD interest आपकी total income में add होता है। अगर total income ₹2.5 lakh से कम है — no tax। ₹2.5-5 lakh — 5% tax। ₹5-10 lakh — 20% tax। ₹10 lakh+ — 30% tax। TDS: Bank ₹40,000+ (senior ₹50,000+) interest पर 10% TDS काटती है।

FD interest is added to your total income. If total income is below ₹2.5 lakh — no tax. ₹2.5-5 lakh — 5% tax. ₹5-10 lakh — 20% tax. ₹10 lakh+ — 30% tax. TDS: Bank deducts 10% TDS on interest above ₹40,000 (₹50,000 for seniors).

Small Finance Bank FD safe है?Are Small Finance Bank FDs safe?+

RBI regulated हैं और DICGC insurance ₹5 lakh तक है। इससे ज्यादा amount है तो risk बढ़ता है। Higher rates = higher risk। Conservative investors के लिए SBI/HDFC/ICICI better है — even at slightly lower rates।

They are RBI regulated and DICGC insurance covers up to ₹5 lakh. Risk increases above this amount. Higher rates = higher risk. SBI/HDFC/ICICI is better for conservative investors — even at slightly lower rates.
