Frequently Asked Questions
EV scooter में battery कितने साल चलती है?How many years does an EV scooter battery last?+

Quality EV brands (Ola, TVS, Ather) की batteries 5-8 साल या 60,000-80,000 km तक reliable रहती हैं। Battery replacement cost ₹20,000-40,000 हो सकती है — इसे 5 साल के बाद consider करें। TVS और Ather की battery warranty 3-5 साल है।

Quality EV brand batteries (Ola, TVS, Ather) remain reliable for 5-8 years or 60,000-80,000 km. Battery replacement cost can be ₹20,000-40,000 — consider this after 5 years. TVS and Ather offer 3-5 year battery warranty.

क्या EV scooter highway पर चल सकता है?Can EV scooters go on highways?+

हाँ — legally हाँ। Ola S1 Pro की top speed 125 km/h है। लेकिन highway पर range बहुत कम हो जाती है — 100 km/h पर range 30-40% कम। Long highway trips के लिए petrol scooter ज्यादा practical है। EV city commute के लिए बेहतर है।

Yes — legally yes. Ola S1 Pro has a top speed of 125 km/h. But range drops significantly on highways — 30-40% less at 100 km/h. Petrol scooter is more practical for long highway trips. EV is better for city commuting.

Ola S1 Pro reliable है?Is Ola S1 Pro reliable?+

Ola ने 2023-24 में quality issues face किए थे। 2025-26 में Gen 3 model के साथ improvements आई हैं। अगर reliability priority है तो TVS iQube या Ather 450X consider करें — इनकी service quality और resale value दोनों बेहतर हैं।

Ola faced quality issues in 2023-24. Improvements have come with the Gen 3 model in 2025-26. If reliability is your priority, consider TVS iQube or Ather 450X — both have better service quality and resale value.

बारिश में EV safe है?Is EV safe in rain?+

हाँ। Quality EVs IP67 या IP65 water resistance rating के साथ आते हैं — normal rain में safe हैं। Deep water crossing से बचें। Charging में extra care लें — wet hands से cable न लगाएं। Overall safety petrol scooter जैसी ही है।

Yes. Quality EVs come with IP67 or IP65 water resistance rating — safe in normal rain. Avoid deep water crossings. Take extra care with charging — don't plug in with wet hands. Overall safety is similar to a petrol scooter.
